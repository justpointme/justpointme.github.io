A Picture is Worth a Thousand Words

JustPoint is a universal translator built by travelers for travelers. Using our app you can easily communicate with anyone, irrespective of what language they speak. Maybe you're in a foreign country craving a beer, or you want to ask how to get to the airport. All you need to do is type the word or concept you want to communicate and we'll provide you with the perfect picture to get your point across. Then, with the picture in hand, just point!



Explain the unexplainable

Direct word translations can help at times, but they can also be quite dangerous. Concepts often get lost in translation, and there is no way to know that what you said is the same as what the other person understood.

Translators such as Google Translate give a false sense of security to our communication which can lead to serious misunderstandings and undesirable events. Additionally they can only translate words, not concepts, and are designed to be a general-purpose tool. On the other hand, JustPoint is made specifically for travelers, aiming to make the experience of visiting a foreign country as smooth and stress-free as possible.

The choice is yours

When travelling, it can be difficult to get access to the internet and there may be limits to how much data you can use. Thus, to provide the best possible experience to users, we offer 3 different options to use our system:

iOS/Android App

The recommended way to use our system. Download the app once and have access to our entire database of pictures offline with an intuitive and simple-to-use UI. (Currently under development)

Online (Web)

Have access to the internet and don't want to download another app? You can siply access the online version of our system, which will use the internet to load for the pictures and provide a nicer UI for using the service.

Offline (Web)

Our offline web system will load all the pictures for you as soon as you access the page so that you can have access to the entire database offline after the page loads once. You can then search for any available picture with no internet at all. To speed up loading times, this service has a simpler UI, ensuring that the user will only load the minimum necessary to run the app.
