Privacy Policy



JustPoint does not use your data in any way. In fact, we cannot get our hands on your data. Currently, our app works entirely offline, so there is no way for us to keep track of anything you do on it. We store your recent searches in your phone itself, and therefore, need to provide you with this privacy policy. The searches never leave your phone and we cannot see them. If any of this changes in the future, we'll be sure to let you know.
